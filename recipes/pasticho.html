Pasticho

Description

Pasticho is a delicious and hearty dish, similar to Italian lasagna but with a unique twist. Layers of pasta, creamy béchamel sauce, and flavorful ground meat come together to create a comforting and satisfying meal. Topped with melted cheese, it's a perfect dish for family gatherings or special occasions.

This recipe is rich and indulgent, combining savory meat sauce and creamy textures to achieve a perfect balance of flavors. It's an excellent choice when you want to prepare something filling and homemade, with a taste that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 12 sheets of lasagna pasta
  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese (optional)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
