Pork Loin

Description

Pork loin is a tender and juicy cut of meat that is perfectly seasoned and roasted to perfection. Its mild flavor and succulent texture make it a versatile dish that pairs well with a variety of seasonings, sauces, and side dishes.

This recipe is simple yet flavorful, resulting in a golden, crispy crust on the outside and a moist, tender interior. Whether grilled, roasted, or slow-cooked, this dish is perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork loin
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme or rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth or white wine (for roasting)
