Lemon Pie

Description

Lemon Pie is a classic dessert that combines a buttery, crisp crust with a smooth, tangy lemon filling and a light, airy meringue or whipped cream topping. Its perfect balance of sweetness and citrus makes it a refreshing treat for any occasion.

This recipe is easy to prepare and delivers a rich, creamy texture with a zesty kick. Whether served chilled or at room temperature, each bite melts in your mouth, making it an irresistible choice for lemon lovers.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made or homemade pie crust
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • 3 egg whites (for meringue, if desired)
  • 1/4 cup sugar (for meringue)
